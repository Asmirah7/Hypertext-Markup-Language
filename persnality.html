Famous

Muhammad Ali was a three-time world heavyweight boxing champion and one of the greatest boxers in history. He was known for his speed, footwork, and ability to take a punch.

Boxing Record

Ali won the world heavyweight championship three times, and his professional record was 56 wins and 5 losses. He was known for his distinctive style, powerful punches, and speed with his hands and feet.
